Rooftop Run

Thrilling 3D Parkour Running Game

About Rooftop Run

Rooftop Run is an exciting parkour-style running game with stunning 3D graphics. Jump from one roof to another, navigate through challenging obstacles, and avoid enemies as you race across the city skyline. Your character runs automatically, so focus on timing your jumps and movements perfectly!

Game Features

3D Graphics

Immersive 3D visuals bring the rooftop parkour experience to life with detailed environments.

Auto-Run Gameplay

Your character runs continuously - focus on jumping, sliding, and avoiding obstacles.

Dynamic Obstacles

Navigate narrow tower cranes, slide under barriers, and dive into office spaces.

Enemy Avoidance

Watch out for orange men enemies and use zigzag patterns to escape them.

How to Play

  • Drag: Move the camera to look around
  • Tap/Click: Jump between rooftops
  • Swipe Down: Slide under obstacles
  • Timing: Perfect your jumps to land safely on the next rooftop

The key to success is anticipating upcoming obstacles and timing your actions. Watch for visual cues and be ready to jump or slide at the right moment.

Tips for High Scores

Stay focused on the path ahead and use the camera controls to spot upcoming obstacles early. When encountering orange men enemies, don't panic - use zigzag movements to avoid them. Practice makes perfect in Rooftop Run, so keep playing to improve your reflexes and learn the patterns!
